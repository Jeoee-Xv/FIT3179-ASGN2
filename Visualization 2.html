Forest Fire

Forest fire is one of the most serious natural disaster, it will burn the forest, reduce the use value of the forest, and destroy the forest ecosystem. Moreover, it will burn down all kinds of buildings and threaten the safety of villages and cities nearby the forest.

Australia is a country that suffer from forest fire almost every year. Becasue Australia's climate is general hot and dry, this is very prone for forests to burn

Brightness (MODIS Brightness Temperature)

The temperature measured in Kelvin calculated from the top-of-the-atmosphere radiances it can clearly show the temperature differences with the surrounding area.

FRP(Fire Radiative Power)

It quantifies burned biomass and measures the radiant energy released per time unit by burning vegetation.
